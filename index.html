How to Sign In to Your TotalAV Account and Update Your Security Settings

TotalAV is a popular antivirus and security software designed to protect your devices from malware, phishing, and other online threats. But owning the software is only half the battle — you need to know how to Sign In to Your TotalAV Account to manage your subscription, download updates, and adjust your security settings for optimal protection. Whether you’re a first-time user or returning customer, logging in to your account ensures you have full control over your protection.

In this guide, we’ll cover everything from the basics of Sign In to Your TotalAV Account, recovering login details, accessing your security dashboard, and customizing settings to suit your needs.

Why Signing In to Your TotalAV Account Matters

When you Sign In to Your TotalAV Account, you’re not just logging into a website — you’re gaining access to the control center for your entire digital security system. Through the account portal, you can:

  • View your subscription details and expiry date
  • Download and install TotalAV on new devices
  • Adjust firewall and web protection settings
  • Manage your password vault and VPN
  • Activate new features included in your plan
  • Contact support directly from your account dashboard

If you skip logging in, you could miss important updates or fail to activate your premium features.

How to Sign In to Your TotalAV Account

Here’s the basic login process:

  1. Open a web browser and go to https://my.totalav.com.
  2. Click on the Log In button.
  3. Enter your registered email address and password.
  4. Click Sign In to access your account dashboard.

For mobile users, download the TotalAV app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, then use the same credentials to log in.

Recovering Your TotalAV Login Information

If you forget your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click Forgot Password?
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Follow the password reset link sent to your inbox.
  4. Create a new password and sign in again.

If you’ve lost access to your email, you’ll need to contact TotalAV Support to verify your identity.

Navigating the TotalAV Dashboard

After you Sign In to Your TotalAV Account, you’ll land on the dashboard, where you can see your subscription status and active features. From here, you can:

  • Run Quick or Full Scans to check for malware.
  • Enable WebShield to block malicious websites.
  • Activate VPN to browse privately.
  • Manage Device Licenses to install protection on additional devices.

Updating Your Security Settings

One of the key reasons to Sign In to Your TotalAV Account is the ability to personalize security settings. Here’s what you can do:

  1. Adjust Real-Time Protection: Make sure real-time scanning is turned on to detect threats instantly.
  2. Customize WebShield Filters: Block phishing websites, dangerous downloads, and unwanted pop-ups.
  3. Set Automatic Updates: Enable automatic updates so your virus definitions are always current.
  4. Configure Firewall Settings: Fine-tune your firewall to control incoming and outgoing network traffic.
  5. Manage Password Vault: Securely store and autofill your login credentials for websites.

Benefits of Updating Security Settings Regularly

Cyber threats evolve every day. Even if you have strong antivirus protection, it’s essential to adjust your security settings regularly. By doing this, you can:

  • Respond to new types of malware and phishing scams
  • Improve performance by optimizing scan schedules
  • Strengthen privacy by updating VPN settings
  • Ensure compatibility with new device software updates

Using TotalAV on Multiple Devices

If your subscription allows multiple devices, you can add them easily:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Manage Devices.
  3. Send an installation link to your other device via email.
  4. Install TotalAV and log in using the same account.

This ensures all your devices are protected under the same plan.

Troubleshooting Login Problems

If you can’t Sign In to Your TotalAV Account, try these fixes:

  • Make sure Caps Lock is off when typing your password.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Disable VPN temporarily if it’s blocking login.
  • Try logging in from another device or browser.

If none of these work, contact TotalAV Support.

Security Tips for Your TotalAV Account

  • Use a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  • Avoid logging in from public computers or unsecured networks.

Conclusion

Learning how to Sign In to Your TotalAV Account and update your security settings is essential for staying safe online. By regularly logging in, you can keep your antivirus up-to-date, fine-tune your settings, and ensure all your devices are fully protected.
